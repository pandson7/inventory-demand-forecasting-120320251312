# JIRA Stories Summary - Inventory Demand Forecasting MVP

## Project Information
- **Project**: echo-architect (EA)

## Created User Stories

### 1. Historical Sales Data Upload and Validation (EA-2196)
- **Type**: Story
- **Labels**: mvp, data-management, upload
- **Description**: Enable store managers to upload and manage historical sales data with validation and quality metrics
- **Key Features**: CSV validation, data integrity checks, error messaging, quality summaries

### 2. Product Catalog Management System (EA-2197)
- **Type**: Story
- **Labels**: mvp, product-management, catalog
- **Description**: Maintain product catalog with categories and attributes for better forecasting accuracy
- **Key Features**: CRUD operations, audit trail, search/filtering, field validation

### 3. Demand Forecasting Engine (EA-2198)
- **Type**: Story
- **Labels**: mvp, forecasting, core-feature
- **Description**: Generate demand forecasts for specific products and time periods using historical data
- **Key Features**: Forecasting algorithms, confidence intervals, data validation, error handling

### 4. Forecast Visualization and Reporting Dashboard (EA-2199)
- **Type**: Story
- **Labels**: mvp, visualization, reporting, dashboard
- **Description**: Display forecasting results in interactive charts and downloadable reports
- **Key Features**: Interactive charts, downloadable reports, comparative analysis, performance metrics

### 5. Inventory Recommendations Engine (EA-2200)
- **Type**: Story
- **Labels**: mvp, recommendations, inventory-optimization
- **Description**: Provide inventory recommendations based on demand forecasts to optimize stock levels
- **Key Features**: Order quantity calculations, reorder points, seasonal adjustments, justification
